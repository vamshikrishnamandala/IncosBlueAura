Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a complete home interior project?

A complete home interior project typically takes 45-90 days depending on the size and complexity. We provide a detailed timeline during consultation and ensure timely delivery.

Do you provide warranty on your interior work?

Yes, we provide up to 10 years warranty on our modular products and 1-year warranty on installation and service work.

Can I customize the designs according to my preferences?

Absolutely! We specialize in bespoke designs tailored to your specific requirements, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences.

What is included in your interior design package?

Our packages include design consultation, 3D visualizations, material procurement, execution, installation, and post-installation support.

Do you handle commercial interior projects?

Yes, we have extensive experience in commercial interiors including offices, retail spaces, restaurants, and hospitality projects.
